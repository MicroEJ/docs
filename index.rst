MicroEJ

Welcome to MicroEJ developer documentation.
Browse the following chapters to familiarize yourself with MicroEJ Technology and understand the principles of app and platform development with MicroEJ.

- The :ref:`Glossary <chapter-glossary>` chapter describes MicroEJ terminology
- The :ref:`Overview <chapter-overview>` chapter introduces MicroEJ products and technology
- The :ref:`Application Developer Guide <application-developer-guide>` presents Java applications development and debugging tools
- The :ref:`Platform Developer Guide <platform-developer-guide>` teaches you how to integrate a C Board Support as well as simulation configurations
- The :ref:`Kernel Developer Guide <kernel-developer-guide>` introduces you to advanced concepts, such as partial updates and dynamic app life cycle workflows
